About Andrew L. Nash, MD FAAP

Professional Journey

With over 30 years of experience in pediatric medicine, Andrew L. Nash has dedicated his career to providing exceptional care for children. As the founder of both 1-to-1 Pediatrics and HouseCall for Kids, Dr. Nash brings extensive expertise in pediatric care, ADHD management, and innovative telemedicine solutions. His vision is to make expert pediatric care accessible to families throughout California through secure virtual visits.

Education & Training

Dr. Nash graduated from Harvard University and earned his medical degree from UC Davis School of Medicine. Dr. Nash completed his pediatric internship and residency at UCSF and Children's Hospital and Research Center, Oakland. His decades of experience span primary care pediatrics, behavioral health, and the development of innovative healthcare delivery systems that prioritize patient convenience and clinical excellence.

Virtual Care Innovation

Pioneering the integration of telemedicine in pediatric care, Dr. Nash has developed platforms that combine the latest technology with compassionate medical care. HouseCall for Kids represents his commitment to providing immediate, expert pediatric attention while eliminating the stress and inconvenience of traditional urgent care visits.

The HouseCall Approach

Dr. Nash's vision for HouseCall for Kids stems from decades of pediatric experience and a deep understanding of families' needs. We combine cutting-edge telemedicine technology with compassionate, expert care to bring pediatric urgent care directly to your home.

Having served thousands of families throughout his career, Dr. Nash recognizes that urgent medical situations require both immediate attention and a calming, reassuring approach. Our virtual platform ensures your child receives expert care without the stress of waiting rooms or travel.

Our approach emphasizes accessibility, excellence, and the personal touch that has defined Dr. Nash's practice for over 30 years. We're committed to helping children heal quickly while supporting families through every step of their healthcare journey.
