About HouseCall for Kids

Dedicated to providing compassionate, professional pediatric urgent care through secure video visits

Our Mission

HouseCall for Kids is committed to delivering exceptional pediatric urgent care services through secure video visits. We understand that when your child is sick or injured, the last thing you want to do is navigate crowded emergency rooms or urgent care clinics.

Our Team

Our experienced pediatric medical professionals are dedicated to providing the highest quality virtual care with compassion and expertise. Each team member is specially trained in pediatric telemedicine and child-friendly virtual care approaches.

What We Treat

Acute Conditions: URIs, ear infections, sinus infections, coughs, fever, and other common pediatric illnesses.

Behavioral & Parenting Concerns: Sleep problems, tantrums, feeding issues, and other behavioral challenges.

Why Choose Virtual Care?

  • Professional medical care from home
  • Experienced pediatric specialists
  • Reduced exposure to other illnesses
  • Convenient scheduling
  • No travel or waiting room time
  • Secure HIPAA-compliant video platform

Our Technology

We use OpenEMR, a secure electronic medical records system, to manage your child's health information. Through our patient portal, you can schedule appointments, access medical records, and communicate with our care team securely.
