About HouseCall for Kids

HouseCall for Kids provides convenient, professional pediatric urgent care services through secure video visits. Our experienced medical team is dedicated to ensuring your child receives expert medical care from the comfort of your home.

We understand that visiting emergency rooms or urgent care clinics can be stressful and time-consuming. That's why we offer virtual visits that connect you with pediatric specialists quickly and safely.
