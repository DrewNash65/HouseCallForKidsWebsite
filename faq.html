Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

What conditions do you treat?

We treat URIs, ear infections, sinus infections, coughs, fever, and other common pediatric illnesses. We also address behavioral/parenting concerns such as sleep problems and tantrums. For life-threatening emergencies, please call 911.

How do virtual visits work?

Virtual visits are conducted through secure video conferencing. You'll need a smartphone, tablet, or computer with camera and internet connection. We'll send you a link to join the video call at your scheduled time.

How quickly can I get an appointment?

We offer same-day appointments for urgent cases. You can schedule through our patient portal or by calling our office. Appointment availability depends on current demand.

Do you accept insurance?

We are out-of-network for commercial insurance plans. Payment is required at the time of service via credit card or HSA/FSA card. We can provide documentation for you to submit to your insurance for potential reimbursement.

What are your visit fees?

Standard 15-minute urgent care visits are $150. Extended 30-minute visits for complex issues are $250. Electronic prescriptions are included with the visit fee.

What ages do you treat?

We provide virtual care for children from birth through 17 years of age.

How do prescriptions work?

Our providers can send electronic prescriptions directly to your preferred pharmacy after the virtual visit. Prescription costs are separate from the visit fee.

Is the patient portal secure?

Yes, our patient portal is powered by OpenEMR and is HIPAA-compliant. Your personal health information is protected with industry-standard security measures.
