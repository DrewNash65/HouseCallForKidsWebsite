Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

What conditions do you treat?

We treat a wide range of pediatric urgent care conditions including fever, respiratory infections, minor injuries, dehydration, allergic reactions, and more. For life-threatening emergencies, please call 911.

What areas do you serve?

We currently serve the greater metropolitan area. Please contact us to confirm we serve your specific location.

How quickly can you come to my home?

We aim to arrive within 1-2 hours for urgent cases, depending on current demand and your location.

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we accept various insurance plans. Please contact us to verify your specific coverage and discuss payment options.

What ages do you treat?

We provide care for children from infancy through age 18.

What equipment do you bring?

Our medical team brings essential diagnostic equipment including otoscope, stethoscope, basic lab supplies, and commonly used medications.
